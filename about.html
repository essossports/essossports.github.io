About ESSOS

Building champions through world-class training facilities

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ESSOS emerged from a vision to provide professional-grade training facilities for cricket and football enthusiasts. What started as a single facility has grown into two state-of-the-art training centers serving hundreds of athletes daily.

Our commitment to excellence and passion for sports development has made us the preferred choice for both amateur and professional athletes in the region.

Our Mission

To provide world-class training facilities and expert coaching that empowers athletes to reach their full potential in cricket and football.
